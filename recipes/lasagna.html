Lasagna

Description

This is a faster and less expensive lasagna!

Nutrition Facts Per Serving: 643 calories; protein 41.3g; carbohydrates 53.4g; fat 29.3g; cholesterol 108.3mg; sodium 707.3mg.

Ingredients

  • 1 package lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1/2 pound shredded mozzerella
  • 1/2 pound shredded cheddar
  • 1 pint ricotta cheese

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain
  2. Preheat oven to 350F. IN a large skillet over medium-high heat, brown beef and season with salt and pepper; drain. Stir in spaghetti sauce and garlic and simmer for 5 minutes.
  3. In a medium bowl, combine mozzarella, Cheddar chees mixture until pan is filled.
  4. Bake in preheated oven for 30 minutes, or until cheese is melted and bubbly.
